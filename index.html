About

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Washington, working with Steven M. Seitz, Jason Lawrence, and Ricardo Martin Brualla. I am interested in combining virtual/augmented reality with computer vision and graphics to produce creative visual experiences. My current research focuses on 3D vision and computational photography.
I am fortunate to have spent great summers interning at Google, Disney Research Zurich, and Facebook. Prior to UW, I received B.S. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University working with Hongtao Lu and visited the National Univerity of Singapore working with Shuicheng Yan.
I am currently on the job market. I would love to chat about any potential opportunities so please reach out!
